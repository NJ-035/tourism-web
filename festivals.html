Explore Tarlac

FOOD

Discover Tarlac’s rich culinary scene, where local flavors and traditions come alive! From the savory Bagis and crunchy Chicharon Camiling to the sweet delights of Tupig (grilled rice delicacy), every bite tells a story. Savor farm-to-table freshness, indulge in authentic Kapampangan-Tarlac fusion, and experience must-visit food spots that define the province’s gastronomic charm. Whether you're craving comfort food or unique delicacies, Tarlac offers a feast for every foodie!

MICROTEL BY WYNDHAM TARLAC

For a memorable, affordable vacation, book your stay at our Microtel by Wyndham Tarlac. Our clean and accommodating guest rooms were designed with your comfort in mind, featuring thoughtful amenities to brighten your stay. Within walking distance from the hotel is The Aquino Center and Museum, which features spacious function rooms, a ballroom, and museum of the late President Corazon Aquino and Senator Benigno Aquino, Jr. You can also enjoy a round of golf at the nearby Luisita Golf & Country Club.
